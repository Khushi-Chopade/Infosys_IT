Company Introduction

Infosys Limited is a leading multinational information technology company from India. It is globally recognized for providing high-quality IT services, software development, and digital solutions. Infosys plays an important role in helping businesses adopt modern technologies and improve their operational efficiency. The company focuses on delivering innovative and sustainable solutions that help organizations grow in a competitive digital world. Infosys follows strong ethical values and maintains transparency, trust, and professionalism in all its operations. History and Foundation Infosys was founded in the year 1981 by seven engineers with a vision to create a world-class IT services organization from India. The company started with a small investment but grew steadily through dedication, technical excellence, and customer satisfaction. Over the years, Infosys has become one of the most respected IT companies in the world.

Vision and Mission

Vision: To become a globally respected corporation that provides best-in-class business solutions through innovation and technology.

Mission: To help clients achieve their business objectives by providing IT solutions that are reliable, efficient, and cost-effective, while maintaining high ethical standards.

Global Presence:


Infosys operates in more than 50 countries across the world, including the United States, Europe, Asia, and Australia. The company serves clients from various industries such as banking, healthcare, education, manufacturing, retail, and telecommunications. With development centers and offices across different continents, Infosys provides continuous support and services to its global customers.

Core Values

    Infosys follows strong corporate values that guide its success:
  1. Integrity and transparency
  2. Customer satisfaction
  3. Innovation and excellence
  4. Respect for employee
  5. Social responsibility

These values help Infosys maintain long-term relationships with clients and employees.
